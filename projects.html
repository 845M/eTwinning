Projects

Every project below pairs our class with a partner school abroad around one shared question. Some run a single term, others carry on for years — replace these with your own project details.

In progress

Active projects

Active · 2025–2026

Skies Over Two Cities

🇫🇮 Vaasa, Finland · Ages 10–11

Two classes log daily weather and compare climate patterns across latitudes, publishing a joint monthly bulletin in English.

Active · 2025–2026

Tales We Both Tell

🇵🇹 Braga, Portugal · Ages 12–13

Students trade folk stories from home, then rewrite each other's tale in their own language and illustrate it for a shared anthology.

Active · Spring term

Market Day, Two Ways

🇬🇷 Chania, Greece · Ages 9–10

Students compare local markets and traditional recipes, ending with a shared cookbook of family recipes translated both ways.

Wrapped up

Completed projects

Completed · 2024

Kitchen Table Science

🇵🇱 Kraków, Poland · Ages 11–12

A joint video series of simple physics experiments filmed in each other's kitchens, narrated in two languages.

Completed · 2023

Letters to a Stranger

🇪🇸 Valencia, Spain · Ages 13–14

A term-long pen-pal exchange that became a printed booklet of letters, translated and bound by both classes.

Completed · 2023

Our Rivers, Their Rivers

🇩🇪 Freiburg, Germany · Ages 10–11

Students mapped local rivers and wildlife, comparing ecosystems and building a shared digital map of both watersheds.
